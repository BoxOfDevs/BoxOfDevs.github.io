Our new website!

  • March 2017

Our new website uses a free template! It features a well designed main page with a working contact form at the bottom if you would like to request a plugin or if you have a question! There is also a beautiful projects page by @Ad5001 which showcases all of our plugins, and has a wiki for usage of all of them! Give feedback on twitter!

Our website beta goes live!

  • February 27, 2017

Our new website has just been uploaded to Github! Our contact form does not work yet, nor does the projects page, but that will all be done within the next month, as our website switches over to a VPS!

Good news, and Bad News

  • February 17, 2017

Unfortunately, one of our founding members (@remote_vase) has quit development and moved on to teaching other people how to code. One of his students is @TheRoyalBlock, our newest member! On twitter, we posted: "Don't worry, @TheRoyalBlock may seem new and unknown, but he's learning from one of our devs who unfortunately just left -> @remote_vase :sad face with tear:"

Development of BoxCore starts!

  • February 6, 2017

A few days ago, one of our members (@remote_vase) suggested that we make an all-in-one core plugin! It would combine many other plugins (if their licence allows) into one major core plugin, along with a few unique features! This is our newest plugin with lots of potential.
